About This Project

This project by Team CodeBlooded is a data visualization tool that helps users explore Spotify's Top Hits from 2000-2019. We use interactive visualizations to help you understand how musical features change across time and how different attributes like popularity, energy, and danceability relate to each other.

Dataset Attributes

• artist: Name of the artist who performed the track.
• song: Title of the song.
• duration_ms: Length of the track in milliseconds.
• explicit: Indicates if the song has explicit content (unsuitable for children).
• year: Year the song was released.
• popularity: A score from 0–100 showing how popular the track is.
• danceability: Measures how suitable a track is for dancing (0.0 to 1.0).
• energy: Describes the intensity and activity of the track (0.0 to 1.0).
• key: Musical key of the track (e.g., 0 = C, 1 = C♯/D♭, ..., -1 = unknown).
• loudness: Average loudness of the track in decibels (dB), ranging from -60 to 0.
• mode: Indicates modality — major (1) or minor (0).
• speechiness: Detects spoken word content (closer to 1.0 means more speech-like).
• acousticness: Confidence score that the track is acoustic (0.0 to 1.0).
• instrumentalness: Predicts whether the track contains no vocals (0.0 to 1.0).
• liveness: Indicates if the track sounds like it was recorded live (0.0 to 1.0).
• valence: Describes the emotional positivity of a track (0.0 = sad, 1.0 = happy).
• tempo: Estimated tempo in beats per minute (BPM).
• genre: The musical genre the track belongs to.
